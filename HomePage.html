CGPA

Cumulative Grade Point Average(CGPA).
You Can Calculate Your Cumulative Grade Point Average. By Clicking on the CGPA CALCULATOR button.CGPA Stands for Cumulative Grade Points Average. ... You can calculate CGPA by adding the grade points of main subjects and excluding additional subjects. Divide the sum obtained with the number of semesters and then you get the CGPA.

SGPA

Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA).
You Can Calculate Your Cumulative Grade Point Average. By Clicking on the SGPA CALCULATOR button. CGPA Stands for Cumulative Grade Points Average. ... You can calculate SGPA by multiplying the credits for the respective subjects and sum the subjects . Divide the sum obtained with the total number of credits and then you get the SGPA.

Percentag

You may Calculate your percentage by summin up your marks scored and dividing it by the maximum number of marks that can be obtained and multiply it by 100 and then you will get your percentage.
